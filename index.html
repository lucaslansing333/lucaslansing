On the practice

For nearly a decade, Lucas Lansing has worked privately with founders, executives, families, and partners navigating the inner cost of a high-output life. The work draws from the Kriya Yoga and Ananda Meditation lineage of Paramhansa Yogananda, alongside somatic, nutritional, and movement practices developed across fifteen years of teaching.

Clients come for clarity, nervous system repair, and a relationship with their own life that no longer runs on borrowed energy.

Three forms of engagement.

Each engagement is held privately, by Lucas and Callie Lansing together, for a small number of clients each year. Length, location, and design are calibrated to the client and the season they are in.

I.

The Concierge Engagement

A six-month private engagement for individuals seeking sustained, integrated work across meditation, somatic health, nutrition, partnership, and inner life. Three intensives anchor the engagement; weekly sessions and asynchronous access hold what unfolds between them.

Learn more
II.

Private Retreats

Three to five days, designed and held for one client, one couple, one family, or a small private group. Conducted by Lucas and Callie at our home studio, at the client’s residence, or at a Tahoe rental prepared for the retreat.

Learn more
III.

Family & Founder Programs

Ongoing engagements for families, family offices, and founder-led companies seeking sustained contemplative and somatic support across a year or more. Annual retainer; bespoke by design.
